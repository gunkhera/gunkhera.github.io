GUNTAAS KHERA

Sulekh

Branding, Identity Design

APPROACH

The task at hand was to create the aesthetic that can reflect the personality of Sumit Khire. The strategy was to connect different aspects of Sumit- what he does, how he is, et al; all into a tangible output which he can further use to promote himself as a graphic designer with core competencies in type design. Sumit's distinct personality needed more than just a mark, something dynamic and bold enough that could spell it all- while representing his diverse skillset and personality. As with any good identity, it had to be timeless and transcend boundaries. It needed to be simple, but be dynamic enough to be seen and recognized easily.

ROLE
Strategy, Identity, Visual Design
